History Emergent

Help us write a story about the future of Human existence.

How It Works:

We are currently creating a record of noteworthy events in the future to serve as canon on which contributors can base their stories.

These will consist of:

  • Global Events
  • Natural Disasters
  • Cultural Movements
  • Technological Advances
  • And more...

There will also be three distinct periods of significance:




Pre-War (2020-2142)

What do people believe in if not their history or religion?

Seventy percent of the population believes in nothing. Religions are forgotten from lack of interest. History is manipulated and new systems of belief are born from and corporate interests. True history becomes a religion all its own.

The world is saturated to the point where few new accomplishments have meaning. It's all been done.

Overpopulation leads to its inescapable conclusion. Education and wealth inequality skyrocket. Knowledge becomes the most valuable resource.

Apathy erodes the human spirit.

But as it is said, a power can only rule so long as it doesn't take everything away. This includes conflict. Without at least the illusion of conflict we are without identity. When deprived of identity, an anger will rise and swell and overcome all obstacles.

WWIII (2142-2150)

The war lasts many years. It is a war that rivals the Peloponnesian and Grecian wars in essence.

A war that last until it is forgotten why the war began.

The spectre of biological warfare and chemical weapons returns, and from it, a new disease emerges amongst the boundless fighting. A disease so cruel it necessitates a global reboot.
