History Emergent

Help us write a story about the future of Human existence.

How It Works:

Currently we have a timeline, a record of noteworthy events in the future, what we need now is stories and art to fill this timeline - to flush it out.

Simply put, we need your help.

While we are eager to accept all forms of art, at this time the website is only equipped to handle documents (.doc, .docx, and .pdf). Pending updates, other types of submissions must be delivered via email to kylewmoser@gmail.com. From there they will be handled with care and published asap.

The timeline is collaborative and dynamic. It will continue to grow and evolve; as your stories and ideas are submitted they will be added, as will all art.

The timeline itself consists of major events such as:

  • Natural Disasters
  • Cultural Movements
  • Technological Advances
  • Etc.

These events effect the course of things to come, and as such they would ideally have a presence in the stories we write and influence the art we create.

In the timeline there will are three distinct periods of significance:

Pre-War (2020-2142)

As humanity continues down this path that has been laid before us we are likely to find a near balance of triumph and tragedy that cripples us with deluded confidence and debilitating comfort.

The acceleration of technological discoveries and advancements is exponential. Religions of today are tomorrows mythologies. Corporate interests take unabashed control of global politics and a secret initiative, codenamed Omnipotence, is founded. Omnipotence endeavors to contort History and spawn new philosophies and systems of belief for a misguided perception of the greater good. Breakthroughs in educational practices improve global intellect dramatically, but the focus with which individuals are taught is just as much a blessing as it is a curse. Education and wealth inequality skyrocket. Knowledge becomes the most valuable resource and it is controlled as such.

True history becomes a religion all its own.

The world is saturated to a point where achievements that would be considered grand in our time, are now common place.

True Artificial Intelligence is born and is guided by the goal of making life as easy as possible for mankind. Conflict and suffering are minimized.

Apathy soon begins to erode the human spirit.

As with all dystopian stories, all seems well initially but before long a movement stirs in the calm. The human spirit lives. It is said, a power can only rule as long as it doesn't take everything away. This includes conflict. Without at least the illusion of conflict we are without identity. When deprived of identity, people will rise and their purpose will swell to overcome all obstacles.

WWIII (2142-2150)

Heroic figures emerge from the populous to begin a revolution opposing these seemingly perfecting systems. The revolution rapidly escalates into war.

It is a war that rivals the Peloponnesian and Grecian wars in essence. A war that last until it is largely forgotten why the war began.

The spectre of biological warfare and chemical weapons returns, and from it, a new disease emerges amongst the boundless fighting. A disease so cruel as to necessitate a global reboot.

Post War (2150-3000)

The trajectory of human wisdom continues forth but, as is the case with progressive movements of all scales, there is always a requisite period of consolidation.

Much knowledge of history and science is lost or scattered along with the diverse souls of the planet and the machines that cared for them.

Decades after the war the soot settles, civilization rises...
